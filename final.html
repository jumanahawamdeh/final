Tourist areas in Jordan

Aqaba

Aqaba is the only coastal city in Jordan and is known for being an attractive area for
diving enthusiasts with its beaches overlooking the Red Sea and a major tourist attraction
in the region. As well as beautiful places to visit, Aqaba is an area full of tourists who
come to enjoy the wonderful sea and beautiful atmosphere.

composition of its waters is different from natural waters, and is one of the most important
tourist areas for ecological healing in the world where it is characterized by a range of natural
factors It has a competitive position in the region in the field of medical tourism and hospitalization
to its unique climatic characteristics, whether it is free of moisture and containing sulfurous
eyes that made it qualified to treat many diseases.

Dhana

It is the first Jordanian heritage village to be restored for tourism. It is a series of valleys and

considered one of the largest nature reserves in Jordan. Mixed with red and white, surrounded
by dense green forests, sometimes extending to hills and plains that are accompanied by gravel
and wonderful golden sand .

Petra

It is an ancient and historical city located in the Governorate of Ma'an. It has been named the "Pink City"
due to the colors of its twisted rocks. Petra is today a symbol of Jordan and the most attractive place
for tourists in the Kingdom. It is also one of the most important tourist destinations for world leaders.
